The Four Types of Intelligence

Our comprehensive training programs focus on developing the four types of intelligence or capacities necessary for effective servant leadership.

Mental Intelligence

Our programs develop the vision of a better future, helping leaders understand complex challenges and create innovative solutions for sustainable development in the Democratic Republic of Congo.

Learn More

Physical Intelligence

We emphasize discipline, orderliness, and motivation. Our training programs help leaders develop the physical and organizational capabilities needed to implement effective governance structures.

Learn More

Emotional Intelligence

Our curriculum focuses on self-control, passion, compassion, collaboration, and interdependency. Leaders learn to manage relationships effectively and work collaboratively for the common good.

Learn More

Spiritual Intelligence

We cultivate conscience, integrity, love, altruism, honesty, generosity, and hospitality. Our programs create leaders grounded in ethical principles and committed to servant leadership.

Learn More
